How To Use

o help you use the system:

Step 1: Registration

  • Click on the "Create New Account" button from the login page.
  • Fill in your registration number, full name, email, password, academic year, phone number, and faculty.
  • Submit the form to register your account.

Step 2: Logging In

  • Enter your Username or Registration Number and Password.
  • Click the "Log In" button.
  • After successful login, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

Step 3: Lodging a Complaint

  • Go to "Lodge New Complaint" from the dashboard.
  • Select the appropriate complaint category.
  • Provide details such as subject, description, and supporting documents (if any).
  • Click Submit to file your complaint.
  • You can't edit and delete any complaint after submitting. So be carefull when submitting!

Step 4: Tracking Complaints

  • Go to "View My Complaints" from the dashboard.
  • Check the status: Pending, In Progress, or Resolved.
  • You can view all details and responses for each complaint.

Step 5: Logging Out

  • Click the "Logout" button at the top right corner of your dashboard to end your session.

Warning

  • You can't edit or delete the complaint after lodging a complaint
  • No notification will be sent to user or admin email address for now

If you face any issues using the platform, please contact the system administrator or your faculty representative.
