About Student Grievance Redressal System

In a university setting, students encounter various challenges related to academics, faculty, administration, infrastructure, and sensitive issues like ragging. The Student Grievance Redressal System is designed to provide a secure, efficient, and transparent platform for students to submit grievances, track their resolution status, and receive timely responses from university authorities.

Key Features

  • Grievance Submission & Categorization: Students can submit grievances under predefined categories for structured resolution.
  • Anonymous Reporting for Ragging Complaints: Special provisions allow students to submit ragging-related grievances confidentially.
  • Status Tracking & Notifications: Real-time updates provide transparency and prompt resolution.

Impact & Expected Outcomes

By streamlining grievance handling, the system fosters accountability, transparency, and student trust, ensuring a safer and more responsive university environment.
